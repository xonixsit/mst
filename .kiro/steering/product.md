# MySuperTax Platform

## Product Overview

MySuperTax is a comprehensive My Super Tax designed for tax professionals to manage client relationships, services, and documentation. The platform consists of two main interfaces:

**Admin Dashboard**: Full-featured management interface for tax professionals to handle client information, invoices, document management, and business analytics.

**Client Panel**: Self-service portal for clients to manage their personal information, upload tax documents, and communicate with their assigned tax professional.

## Core Features

- **Client Information Management**: Comprehensive client data collection including personal details, spouse information, employment records, assets, and expenses
- **Invoice Management**: Create, track, and manage invoices with automated tax calculations and email delivery
- **Document Management**: Secure upload, organization, and access to tax-related documents
- **Communication Center**: Built-in messaging system for client-professional communication
- **Audit Trail**: Complete logging of all client interactions and data changes
- **Bulk Operations**: Efficient management of multiple clients simultaneously
- **Financial Analytics**: Dashboard metrics and reporting for business insights

## Target Users

- **Tax Professionals**: Primary users who manage client accounts, prepare taxes, and run the business
- **Clients**: End users who need tax consulting services and want to manage their information digitally

## Key Business Rules

- All client data must be validated and secured according to tax industry standards
- SSN and sensitive information require special handling and encryption
- Audit trails are mandatory for compliance and accountability
- Multi-section forms organize complex client information logically
- Dynamic tables allow flexible data entry for assets and expenses
